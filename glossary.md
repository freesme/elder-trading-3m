# Glossary

**2%规则** - Maximum risk on any single trade should not exceed 2% of trading capital. (Ch 7)

**6%规则** - Monthly account-level risk brake; stop adding risk when monthly losses plus open risk reach 6% of prior month-end equity. (Ch 7)

**3M** - Mind, Method, Money: psychology, trading method, and money management. (Intro, Ch 4, Ch 7)

**ABC打分系统** - Watchlist triage: A = actionable tomorrow, B = possible this week, C = not currently actionable. (Ch 9)

**Elder-ray / 埃尔德线** - Indicator comparing bull power and bear power with an EMA. (Ch 5)

**MACD柱形图** - Histogram of MACD line distance, used to detect trend strength and reversals. (Ch 5)

**SafeZone / 安全区域止损** - Stop method based on recent adverse noise rather than obvious support/resistance. (Ch 6)

**三重滤网系统** - Multiple-timeframe trading system: strategic direction, tactical setup, precise entry. (Ch 6)

**交易手段** - Specific entry/exit tactic inside a broader system. (Ch 6)

**交易日记** - Visual and written record of trade context, emotions, entry, exit, and lessons. (Ch 8)

**交易系统** - Organized rules for selection, direction, entry, exit, stops, and risk. (Ch 6)

**交易计划** - Written decision tree tailored to market, account, strategy, and personality. (Ch 9)

**价格通道** - Bands around a moving average used to identify stretched price and grade trade capture. (Ch 5, Ch 6)

**仓位规模** - Number of shares/contracts determined by account risk and per-unit risk. (Ch 7)

**假突破** - Break beyond a visible level that fails and traps emotional participants. (Ch 5)

**动量交易** - Trading in the direction of emerging or continuing crowd movement. (Ch 1)

**反趋势交易** - Betting that an emotional stretch will revert; requires strong exits and risk control. (Ch 1)

**基本面分析** - Analysis of supply/demand, business, economics, or commodity drivers. (Ch 3)

**安全区域指标** - Volatility/noise measure used to construct SafeZone stops. (Ch 6)

**开盘价** - First consensus of the session, often influenced by overnight orders. (Ch 5)

**强力指标 / Force Index** - Price change multiplied by volume, connecting direction, magnitude, and participation. (Ch 5)

**支撑位 / 阻力位** - Zones where prior market commitments may affect future behavior. (Ch 5)

**收盘价** - Final consensus of the period and an important institutional reference. (Ch 5)

**日间交易** - Trading completed intraday; requires exceptional focus, liquidity, volatility, and cost control. (Ch 6)

**期望值** - Long-run expected outcome of a trading method. (Ch 7)

**未平仓合约** - Open interest in futures; helps infer commitment of market participants. (Ch 6)

**流动性** - Ability to enter and exit without excessive slippage. (Ch 2)

**滑点** - Loss from execution price differing from intended price. (Ch 2)

**交易者电子表格** - Structured trade log with costs, P/L, entry score, exit score, and trade score. (Ch 8)

**资金曲线** - Graph of account equity over time; objective performance and discipline measure. (Ch 4, Ch 8)

**经营者风险** - Small predefined business risk accepted as a normal cost of trading. (Ch 7)

**袋鼠尾** - Long protruding bar that signals emotional rejection of an extreme. (Ch 5)

**随机指标** - Oscillator showing close position within a recent range; useful for overbought/oversold and divergence. (Ch 5)
